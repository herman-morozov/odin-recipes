Lentil Patties Recipe

Description

These wonderful patties are easy to make and very tasty

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Olive oil
  • Dried onion
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add boiled water to lentils
  2. Add salt
  3. Add dried onion
  4. Let it all set
  5. Make patties
  6. Fry with olive oil
  7. Add tomato sauce before eating
  8. Eat
