Pelmeni Recipe

Description

Pelmeni are east to cook and very tasty, especiallly with a tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pelmeni
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add pelmeni to the boiling water with salt
  2. Cook for 10 minutes
  3. Add olive oil and tomato sauce to the dish
  4. Eat
